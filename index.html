My Long Letter to You

Hi, Eunice this one's a long message for you. Alam mo naman gustong gusto kita at hindi ako madaling sumuko pero kelangan kong i-respeto ikaw pati narin sarili ko. Ayaw ko rin naman masaktan ka. Ang nakikita ko lang talaga hindi pa talaga siguro ngayon.

Grabe talaga kumilos si Lord noh? Biruin mo yun kagabi habang naguusap tayo at pinapatulog na si Vincent, naging anxious sya at di mapakali naka isa, dalawa, tatlong tayo siya na di mapakali. Yung pangatlong tayo nya kumuha sya ng bimpo/towel pamunas at ibinigay sakin without saying anything dun palang nagtaka na ako, bat ko naman ke-kelanganin yun? Then ngayon, I didn't imagine I'd need it dahil nagamit ko ngayon ng sobra sobra yung binigay nyang bimpo/towel. Grabe ka talaga gumalaw Lord.

Maybe in another time, we're going to meet again pero sa time na yun ready na, may mapagmamalaki na, at hindi na takot dahil pareho at parehas na tayong nasa best version ng sarili natin. Siguro nga ayun muna talaga yung nakatakda mangyari noh? Yung pangarap mong sinabi mo saakin na plinano nyo ng kuya mo? Siguraduhin mong kaya mo ahh at tutuparin mo yun, nasa sayo ang buong suporta ko. Aabutin ko din yung sakin. Tandaan mo, hindi ako galit at wala akong galit kahit kakaonti sayo, patawarin mo yung sarili mo dahil pinapatawad kita dahil pareho naman nating ginusto ito, wala tayong kasalanan dalawa sa isa't isa, don't think that it's your fault. What I want you to think is, this past few month we've been together hanging out and having fun together has been the happiest and greatest time of our life, because it is true. You are the best thing that happened in my life. Just trust me. Don't think of anything negative because I wouldn't want that. I'm okay and I truly understand what has happened and why we need to do this. Always remember that everything that I said is true and my will. Just keep my things and don't try to return them, I gave it to you with all my heart. Do what you want with it. I'll take care and protect all the things that you gave me with all my might.

Take care of yourself pu ha? Wag mong pabayaan yung sarili mong masaktan ng kahit na sino-man. Don't pressure yourself too much pu ha, okay? Don't push yourself too hard, take it easy. Rest when you need too. Always pray din pu ha. Lagi mong tatandaan na nandito ako palagi. I hope we can still be friends, I know we can. I'll still remember everything and I will never forget what we've had all this time we spent together. I wish you all the best and happiness. Nakakalungkot talaga Eunice :( I'll wait for you even if it took me long time. I will, pero along the way I'll meet many people. Don't worry uusad ako at hindi naman ako makukulong sayo. Don't think that I'll never move from this position. I'll use this as an inspiration to take the sky, the stars, and the life that I'm dreaming of. I respect you very much and your decision. Sayang eh, gusto ko kasama kita abutin lahat ng pangarap natin na magkasama sana eh. Maybe binibigyan talaga tayo ni Lord ng time para ayusin muna yung pareho natin pangarap at sarili. So that, when the time comes everything is ready na talaga. I want you to OPEN this and ask you.
